About

You can call me Simon!

I am a full-stack problem solver (and amateur conjurer of spirits that live in my computer) based out of Greenpoint, Brooklyn. Whether it is improving front-end performance, collaborating with stakeholders across team boundaries to build new solutions or debugging a pernicious networking error - I enjoy it all and there is nothing else I would rather do professionally. In recent roles, I focused on building applications using React/Redux, Express and Django but I believe in using the best tools for the job so I am always on the lookout for opportunities to learn new technologies. At the moment I am having a blast learning Rust and improving my knowledge of TypeScript.

I hold a Master's degree in Economics from the University of Glasgow and have since completed the Computer Science and Applied Mathematics curriculum at Stony Brook University. I left the program without obtaining the official degree because during the tail end of what proved to be my last semester, I decided to attend App Academy, a wonderfully rigorous web development bootcamp, which proved to be one of the hardest, most rewarding and growth inducing periods of my life. When I am not writing code or learning, I am usually thinking of ways to go freediving or skiing. Feel free to look through some of my projects below or on Github, connect with me on LinkedIn or send me an email at wojnar48@gmail.com to just say hello.
