About

Hi there! I'm Szymon and I like to speak to the spirits that live in my computer.

Recently, I've built projects with Ruby on Rails, JavaScript (React, Redux, jQuery) HTML5, CSS3 and PostgreSQL. I also have experience using Python to make the lives of my team better and filled with less tedium through automation.

Every day I am afflicted with a desire to write elegant code, a good instinct for finding database inefficiencies and off-by-one bugs, an ambition to make a difference for my team and a tendency to drink too much coffee. Thinking about bottlenecks and optimizing performance puts a smile on my face, and is a key reason of why I became a software engineer, apart from wanting to work with team of passionate and curious problem solvers. I hold an MS degree in Economics and have recently completed coursework in Applied mathematics and Computer Science. Feel free to look through my recent projects below or on Github, connect with me on LinkedIn or send me an email at wojnar48@gmail.com. I would love to hear from you!
